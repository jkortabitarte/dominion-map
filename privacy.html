Privacy Policy

Dominion is a fitness visualization application that integrates with the Strava API to generate personal territory maps based on GPS activities.

Data Access

Dominion only accesses Strava activity data strictly necessary to generate visual maps, including activity identifiers and GPS polylines.

Data Usage

Imported data is used exclusively within the application to compute territory influence and display personal statistics. Dominion does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Data Retention

Users may disconnect their Strava account at any time. Upon disconnection, no further data is imported. Previously imported data can be deleted upon request.

User Control

Users remain in full control of their data and may revoke access directly from their Strava account settings or within Dominion.
