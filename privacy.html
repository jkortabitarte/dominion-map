Privacy Policy

Dominion is a fitness-based strategy game that allows users to visualize their activities and territorial influence on a map.

Data collected

When you connect your Strava account, Dominion accesses the following data:

  • Strava athlete ID
  • Public activity data (GPS routes / polylines)

How we use your data

Activity GPS data is used exclusively to calculate territory influence within the Dominion game. No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising.

Data storage

We store only the minimum data required to provide the service. You can disconnect Strava at any time from your profile page.

Disconnecting Strava

You may disconnect your Strava account at any time from the Profile section of the app. This will revoke access to your Strava data.
